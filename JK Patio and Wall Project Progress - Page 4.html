    Attachment 2212

    These are the major things I need to get done before Saturday:
    1. Finish the remaining pavers
    2. Build up the wall and columns
    3. Lay 6x9 pavers along the front border
    4. Add the caps to the wall

    It's 2 pavers short from completing the pattern. They were one paver short in inventory when I picked up the order and I thought I might be able to squeeze by with the right cuts, but I have to pick the extra one up tomorrow. I have the cut piece set aside in the meantime.
